Hi, I'm João Brandão (pronounced: _zhoh-OWN brahn-DOWN_), I am a PhD student in the Operations
Research & Industrial Engineering program at the University of Texas at Austin.

I previously completed a Master's in Engineering Management, where I focused on the calibration
of risk-mitigating mechanisms in public-private partnership (PPP) projects, with applications to
road infrastructure.

Before graduate school, I worked as a software engineer. I was the founding engineer at Lina Open X,
where I built systems for financial banking applications, particularly in OpenBanking.
